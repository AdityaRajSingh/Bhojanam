Donate Food

Feeding India to eradicate hunger, malnutrition and food wastage in India. Once we have your address and a few other details, your donation is as simple as a quick text or phone call.

Recieve Food

A platform where excess cooked food is procured and distributed among hungry as well as needy people. All we need are your aadhar number, and the number of people associated. Just be at the delivery van at the specified time and specified place.

Deliver Food

Delivery boys are the lifeblood of this program. All you need is a car or bike and a phone. We'll provide you with everything else, and will schedule pick ups and deliveries that are as close and convenient for you as possible. Thanks!
